Technical Beauty

Men respect technical beauty because they are evolutionarily designed to do so. As a woman, you carry the unique burden and opportunity of being both muse and object. Whether in a presentation, the way you artfully walk across the room, how you wear your hair, the way that you speak your words, there is a technical Grace that men associated with feminine strength that they respect and honor. Acknowledging this inherited quality of how men perceive women is insightful for understanding why a man likes anything about any woman. It is very likely that and some calculated for technical way your precision and anything ranging from engineering, to cooking, to kindness has drawn him in and made him open to receiving you.
